As the President of ASME at UCR, you will work with the university faculty to lead the way for your organization members and officers. With the officer board, the President’s responsibilities include holding meetings with administration and officers to design, plan, and implement events, research new organization opportunities and collaborations, and be a resource for the ASME at UCR members. Each academic year poses new challenges and opportunities. It is the President’s responsibility to support the organization and continue a standard of excellence.
